Privacy Policy

Nexio is committed to providing our customers and visitor with a website that respects their privacy. This page summarizes the privacy policy and practices for all of Nexio's website divisions. The Nexio website does not automatically gather any personal information from you, such as your name, phone number, or e-mail address. This information is only obtained if you provide it voluntarily, through contacting us or using one of our services, such as our online application form.

Information Collected Automatically Via The Internet

The Nexio web server logs capture computer information, navigation, and click-stream data such as HTTP requests. Some of the information, such as your domain name or IP address, may identify you depending on the naming standards followed by your Internet service provider.

he information captured does identify the following; the Internet domain and IP address from which you access our website, the type of browser and operating system used to access our site, the screen resolution of your monitor, the date and time you access our site, the pages you visit, and if you linked to the Nexio web site from another web site, the address of that web site. This information is used to help us make our site more useful to our audiences by learning about the number of visitors to our site, and by monitoring traffic patterns and the types of technology our visitors use. Nexio also uses this information to track the origin of specific visitors to better understand its marketing efforts. This information is collected by Nexio as well as third-party analytics providers, including the Google Analytics service.

Cookies

A cookie is a small piece of text information that is sent to your browser when you access a website. Your browser will return this cookie information to the domain where the cookie originated. Nexio uses cookies to better track multiple visits made by visitors to our website as well as help you fill in forms. Nexio sets these cookies for a prolonged period of time. The length of time that the cookie will reside on your machine will depend upon the specific set-up of the particular sites you visit. If you are concerned about the use of cookies, you can refer to the help information in your browser software for information on how to disable cookies. If you wish to delete cookies from your machine, consult your browser's help files for instructions on how to do so.

Links to Other Sites

Our website may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the content and the privacy practices of other websites and encourage you to examine each site's privacy policy and disclaimers and make your own decisions regarding the accuracy, reliability, and correctness of material and information found.

Information Collected For Nexio Services

To open a Nexio Merchant account, you will be asked to provide us with specific information about yourself and your business. Some of this information may be shared with a third party, such as a credit check bureau. For more information about how your information is used, please read the Nexio Merchant agreement.

Disclosures Required by Law

We reserve the right to disclose your personal data as required by law and when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights and/or to comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process served on us.

Security of Stored Information

Nexio is committed to handling your information with high standards of information security. We use computer safeguards such as firewalls and data encryption, we enforce physical access controls to our buildings and files, and we authorize access to personal information only to employees who need to know that information in order to fulfill their job responsibilities.
